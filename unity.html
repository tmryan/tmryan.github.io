Game Level in Unity3D

Short demo of content created with Unity3D

This is a small development sandbox project I created while learning Unity3D. The level consists of a chest which can hold any loot item as a GameObject, a standard assets FPS player controller with some scripting for clickable object interactions and weapon handling, and some cool low-poly assets just for fun.

Assets used in this level are a mix of free asset packages and some standard assets. You can find links to them on the asset store at the bottom of this page.
