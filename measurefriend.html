MeasureFriend

MeasureFriend is a simple Android app that makes converting from one common kitchen measurement to another easy.

Frequently when cooking or baking, one runs into the problem of needing to convert from one measurement to another. After repeatedly running to my computer so I could ask Google how many tablespoons there were in a cup, it was obviously time for technology to intervene. Thus, MeasureFriend was born.

If you'd like to see the code, check it out on GitHub.
