About

In January of 2014, I quit my job in the video game industry and dove into school head-on. Now that I've graduated with a Bachelors in Computer Science from San Jose State University, I'm ready to get back to work. I'm interested in all aspects of programming, design, and development but I find game programming and graphics especially interesting.

On top of the knowledge I've garnered in my scholarly pursuits, I have 7 years of software quality assurance experience from my time at Blizzard Entertainment, 4 of which were spent leading a team of amazing QA analysts. At Blizzard, I had the fortune to work closely with many talented people on some of the most awesome products in the industry. Those experiences have molded part of who I am today.

I'm really excited to get back out into the working world and I'm always looking for opportunities to learn and grow.
